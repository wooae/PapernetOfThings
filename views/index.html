The Papernet of Things: About


Our Mission

To protect and preserve the environment by repurposing wasted paper material into reliable products that help improve peoples' lives.

Our Values

  • Passion. We are driven to save the world from being clogged with wasted paper. Repurposing paper helps protect the world's trees.
  • Leadership. We aim to be the best-in-class at what we do.
  • Integrity. Our products will be of exceptional quality and their integrity will never be compromised.
  • Happiness. Our goal is to make products from repurposed paper that people will enjoy and use in their everyday lives.

Saving the World

The United States is the world's top consumer of paper products in the world, with much of this used paper going into landfills as waste. Some of this paper is recycled, but many paper products cannot be recycled such as pizza boxes and coffee cups. We, the Papernet of Things, want to use these currently non-recyclable materials to create new products, thus reducing the amount of paper products that end up in landfills. This semester, we will be focusing on creating cell phone cases out of used pizza boxes.
